About Us

After spending almost 20 years in the trades and watching how companies, jobsites and projects came together and fell apart, I decided my next passion lay in helping my fellow tradesman find his/her success with less stress. Having worked from a newbie in a trade I never even knew existed before a friend suggested it, learning from the ground up and then being headhunted by a company that I would spend the next eleven years helping to build, I have been very fortunate to end up here. I worked all positions through the two shops I was employed withand ended in a bookkeeping and project management role, which allowed me to see so many aspects of the who's, what's, where's, why's and how-the-heck's. I've watched countless contractors of all trades struggle with a day/week/month of nothing goes as planned, knowing at the end of the day they don't just call it a day. The rest of the work happens, the entrepreneur part, the part no one sees, when the shop lights are off and the jobsite is empty, when the time you'd like to spend with your family or hobbies is consumed by the mountains of paperwork and scheduling. I built this for you. I built this so I can do something I'm passionate about in helping the hardworking men and women have moment to breathe while building their dreams, their companies and opportunities for their employees and customers. Born and raised in BC, I have proudly called Alberta home for over two decades. I have set my roots here; it is where my family calls home, and while this is where we are based, Ibis offers services that are accessible and customizable to entrepreneurs across Canada.
